I very recently started messing around with Odin, and I really enjoy the language.
It is frustrating, however, that there are so few educational resources available online,
so I thought I would write some of my own!

*Note: There is a very good [book](https://odinbook.com) available for $20 that I would recommend
for anyone interested in diving deep.  The [docs](https://odin-lang.org/docs/overview/) are also
good!*

I thought to start off, we could look at various ways to do filesystem I/O in Odin, and explore some of the
concepts that come up as we go.
